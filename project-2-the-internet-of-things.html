The Third Industrial Revolution - IoT

System Introduction

Project Overview

Jeremy Rifkin, an economist and political adviser, presents a highly ambitious text that describes some fundamental transformations underway in the world economy, provides a road map to future change and explains why such massive shifts are necessary. Many of the topics Rifkin discusses will be familiar to you, such as the threat of climate change or the potential of new collaborative business models. What Rifkin offers, and what makes his book both exciting and a bit daunting, is an overarching narrative that places technological, social and economic currents in historical and modern contexts.

What I did

While living through the first lockdown, I stumbled across Rifkin's IoT proposal and became obsessed. I have always been interested with renewability, transportation and technology and his proposed system encapsulates all of these into an open source platform. My main takeaway from the book was that I had not heard of this system before; why? It raised the question of who gets to know about this future system and who gets to gain from it? I built an interactive museum experience that families could interact with at home, introducing the real opportunity it holds for society.

Photography Support Capgemini

Event Support

Documenting the events of the Capgemini talent week in Paris.

Project Overview

In 2015, Capgemini University’s global curriculum delivered more than 3.3 million learning hours to over 161,000 participants, which represents 89% of the Group’s headcount. 4,700 were at their learning campus in Les Fontaines, France. 34,000 locally across 40 countries. 136,000 virtually with e-learning modules and in virtual classrooms.

My Contributions

I was fortunate enough to support the events team in various tasks such as build-up, Icons design for future events and photography. I learned how to adapt to the job and and how to work alongside different teams each day. It was a fantastic week-long experience in France and one that will heavily influence my team skills in the future.

Transport for London

Brand Collaboration

How can we boost cycle engagement in London?

Project Overview

When studying at CSM, I was offered the opportunity to work with TFL on a campaign focused on boosting cycling in London as the city began its move to opening various cycle networks.

My Contributions

I began by adding a further focus to the target audience by engaging with the 250,000 students that arrive in the city each year. With so much free data I proposed a TFL Starter Kit. One of the biggest stresses that I had when moving to the city from the country was getting my student oyster card. This welcome kit would include a free 2-hour access card that students would receive in halls so they could get to know their flat and the city they had just moved into. Furthermore, this would then double up as their student oyster card when they began travelling to university.

Jack C Robertson

Access and Infomation Designer

I am London based graphic designer from Cornwall. My design process focuses on introducing audiences to the problematic nature of socio-economic data in the twenty-first century. I am interested in the design infrastructure of information and how research-led curation can create an efficient and cohesive commentary on our thematic understanding of politics, economics and conflict.

Project Overview

In 2021, Western news coverage of governments’ involvement in the Middle East began to fade. I questioned in my dissertation... How can information design introduce and clarify the complex human and political scale of destruction and displacement in the Middle East?After observing that ‘out of sight out of mind’ attitude of a lot of western demographics towards the calamity In the east, I was intrigued to investigate how I could reintroduce and Inspire interest and the ownership of knowledge.

My Contributions

I developed a design system that focussed around communicating the human scale of the conflict in the Middle East. With a refined design language which functioned in two parts. Part one is 70m of hand rendered scrolls representing the total number of homes that have been made uninhabitable by the conflicts. Part two is the making of three books which physically present  the enormity of the astonishing number of people displaced from conflict.
